1 VS 1 FACE BATTLE

Tips and tricks:

  • NEUTRAL: Tighten your lips! Especially if you have thick lips
  • HAPPY: Wide toothy smile!
  • SAD: Turn the corners of your lips downwards and tilt your head up
  • SURPRISED: Easy. Just open your mouth
  • FEARFUL: Widen your eyes and cup your cheeks with your fingers
  • ANGRY: Scrunch up your nose, grit your teeth, and tilt your head down
  • DISGUSTED: Squint your eyes and pull up one corner of your mouth
